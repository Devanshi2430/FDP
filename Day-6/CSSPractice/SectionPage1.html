Gujarat Technological University (GTU) is a state university with 486 affiliated colleges. It is headed by the state government and came into existence on May 16, 2007. GTU is approved by UGC, NBA & PCI for its academic performance and records. It is the first University in Gujarat which uses many Information Technology tools for its major activities. The university has about 4,00,000 students enrolled in various UG, PG, Research and diploma courses. Admission is made on the basis of entrance & merit both based. The university also conducts some entrance tests for admission and accepts few national-level entrance scores too.
